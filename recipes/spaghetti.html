How to make Spaghetti:

Description:

This is a general recipe for spaghetti that avoids a dry consistency and aims for that flavorful experience with a "just-right" approach to flavor. It is a quick and easy recipe that will leave you bewildered at the prospect of how simple it can truly be. After finishing this recipe, you can add it to your arsenal of dishes that not only will feed an entire family but leave them with smiles at the dinner table. Spaghetti is infamous for being a cheap meal to make and delivering doubly on the taste.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Hamburger Meat
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Start boiling a pot of salted water.
  2. Put noodles in boiling water.
  3. Cook your hamburger meat in a non-oiled pan. Breaking it into smaller pieces.
  4. Drain noodles after 20-30 minutes.
  5. Put noodles back in pot with hamburger meat and spaghetti sauce.
  6. Mix thoroughly and top with salt and pepper.
