How to make Bacon:

Description:

Bacon is like a candy made out of meat. The aroma of bacon can allure anyone to hunger, and the prospect of eating bacon is one of the most unimaginably tasty things we can imagine! The best part is that it's incredibly simple to make bacon and it's hard to mess up a good tasting piece of bacon.

Ingredients:

  • Bacon

Steps:

  1. Grab a frying pan and grab your bacon.
  2. Turn the stove heat on high.
  3. Put your bacon on the pan and start frying until well-brown.
  4. Put your bacon on a plate with a towel to soak up the grease and let it cool before enjoying.
