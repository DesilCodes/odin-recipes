How to make french fries:

Description

French fries are a delectable treat enjoyed around the world for their ready availability, good-taste, and meal application! You can go to just about any restaurant in the world and find this item on the menu. There's a reason for that, and it's because it's one of the tastiest snacks that you can enjoy over and over again.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Wash and dry your potatoes.
  2. Peel the potatoes with a peeler.
  3. Dice them into desired french fry cuts on a cutting board.
  4. Start heating up your vegetable oil in a pot.
  5. Put your potato cuts in the hot oil and fry away.
  6. Wait until golden crispy brown and until fries start to float to the top of your oil.
  7. Place fries in a bowl of layered towels to suck the grease and moisture out of the fries.
  8. Enjoy!
